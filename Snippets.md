## Create GitHub EMU Account

Visit the GitHub link and set up your EMU account with your unique username for access to repositories and collaboration.

Goto [https://github.com/enterprises/microsoft](https://github.com/enterprises/microsoft)

Your username will be <alias>_microsoft

[https://code.visualstudio.com/download](https://code.visualstudio.com/download)
